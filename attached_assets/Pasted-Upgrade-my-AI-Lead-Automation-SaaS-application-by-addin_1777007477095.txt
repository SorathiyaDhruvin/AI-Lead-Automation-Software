-------------------------------
1. AI LEAD SCORING & SUMMARY
-------------------------------
Purpose:
This is the core intelligence of the app. The system should automatically analyze each lead and determine how valuable it is.

What to build:
- Analyze lead data (name, email, message, source, activity)
- Generate:
  - score (0–100)
  - category (Hot / Warm / Cold)
  - AI-generated summary
  - recommended next action

Implementation:
- Create API: POST /api/ai/score-lead
- Store results in database inside lead document
- Show score and summary in UI (lead details page)
